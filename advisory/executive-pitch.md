# Executive Pitch

Quilvar is an AI-assisted market signal intelligence system.

It continuously monitors trusted news sources and filings, identifies events likely to move markets or prediction-market odds, structures those events into ranked signals, and alerts the team when something looks material. It then tracks what actually happened over time, creating a feedback loop between detection and real-world outcomes.

The value is not “AI that predicts everything.” The value is a disciplined system that turns noisy information flow into structured, measurable signal intelligence.

## Why It Matters

- it reduces reaction time to market-moving information
- it turns unstructured news into ranked, reviewable signals
- it builds a proprietary dataset of what kinds of events actually mattered
- it creates a foundation for better decision support over time

## What Is Differentiated

The strategic asset is the validation loop.

Most systems can summarize headlines. Quilvar is designed to detect signals, store them, validate their outcomes over 1d, 7d, and 30d, and use that history to improve future judgment. That turns raw monitoring into a compounding internal data asset.

## Current Position

This is a strong prototype with real strategic potential, but it should be positioned today as an internal intelligence copilot, not as a production-grade prediction engine.

The architecture direction is sound. The next requirement is trust: data integrity, clean validation history, correct deduplication, and operational reliability.

## Near-Term Message

Launch it internally as an advisory system that helps the team surface and evaluate market-relevant events faster and more systematically than manual monitoring.
